Visit Toledo

Enjoy culture and sports in this vibrant city in Northwestern Ohio.

Top three activities to do in Toledo

Visit the Toledo Zoo and Aquarium

Get lost in the expansive world of land and aquarian life at the Toledo Zoo.

Emerse yourself in the Toledo Museum of Art

Sample art from all styles and parts of the world at this world reknowned museum.

Enjoy a game at the Fifth-Third Field

Experience the vibrant community of sports in the home of the Mud Hens.
